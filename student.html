Login

Use your school-provided email to login. You can change your password under "Settings".

  1. Check your email for your username and temporary password.
  2. Click the invite link or visit your school's login URL.
  3. Log in and go to "Settings" (top-right) to change your password.

Check Report Cards

Find report cards under the "Reports" tab within "View Complete Record".

  1. Click “View Complete Record”
  2. Scroll down and click “Reports”
  3. Select a report card to view or download

Check Current Grades

  1. Click “View Complete Record”
  2. Scroll to “Gradebook”
  3. View grades or click “Preview” or “Download”

Check Homework

  1. Click “View Complete Record”
  2. Go to the “Homework” tab
  3. Preview or download homework assignments

Verify Student Info

Click “View Complete Record” to review and confirm your personal info.

Request updates from the school if needed.

Check Class Schedule

Use the “Schedule” tab to view your timetable. If unavailable, contact the school.

Use Apps & Modules

  1. Check the menu bar for any additional features/modules
  2. Click on links to explore available tools
  3. Private Message Teachers using the talk bubble at the top right

Private Messaging

Use the talk bubble or Directory to send messages to your teachers securely.

Pay Bills

  1. Click “View and Pay Fees”
  2. Review charges and click “Pay Fees”
  3. Enter credit card details to complete payment

Need Help?

Click the “Got a Question” widget at the bottom right of any page to contact support.
